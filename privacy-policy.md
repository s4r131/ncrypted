# Privacy Policy

**ncrypted** — Last updated: March 2026

## Overview

ncrypted is a zero-server, offline-first encrypted file sharing app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What We Don't Collect

- No accounts or sign-up required
- No analytics or tracking
- No crash reporting
- No advertising identifiers
- No location data

## What Stays on Your Device

- **Your RSA identity keypair** — generated locally and stored in your device's secure enclave (iOS Keychain). Never transmitted.
- **Your contacts' public keys** — stored locally on your device only.
- **Encrypted files** — all encryption and decryption happens on-device. No files are sent to any server.

## Camera Usage

The camera is used solely to scan QR codes for key exchange. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

## File Access

Files you select are read locally for encryption only. ncrypted does not upload, copy, or retain your files.

## Third-Party Services

ncrypted uses no third-party SDKs, analytics, or services that collect data.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
